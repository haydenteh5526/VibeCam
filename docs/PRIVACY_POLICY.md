# VibeCam Privacy Policy (Draft)

Last updated: 2026-04-27

VibeCam is a personal camera app that lets you capture photos or videos and upload them to your own backend.

## Information We Collect

- Media you choose to capture and upload (photos, videos, and audio embedded in videos).
- Basic app diagnostics generated by the device operating system (if any).

## How We Use Information

- To capture and upload your media to your backend.
- To display upload progress and status within the app.

## Data Sharing

We do not share your data with third parties.

## Data Retention

Your media is stored on the backend you control. Retention depends on your backend configuration.

## Your Choices

You can delete media stored on your backend at any time using your backend tools.
